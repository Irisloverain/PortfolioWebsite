Exhibition KV

Eggs in the Universe

This poster KV is for the freshman final exhibition of interaction design department.

2018

Process

Sketch

Initially, when the first time I heard the name of the exhibition, the eggs in the universe, some idea popped up in my mind immediately: sunny side up egg, the universe, spaceman,and the earth. Then, it reminded me another thing: sceinfiction, manga, novel. Finally, I decided the style would be fun and like a page of manga.

Composition

I had try three different kinds of composition because there need more content inlude the post and I need more flexible space. The second one take me a lot of times to made but it seems too squeeze. Hence, I redesigned the layout and use more dymanic frame.

Colour

The final step is deciding the colour. I developed differnt colour scheme and compare each other. Our department also tookplace a vote to choice the colour.

Final

The story is about a egg escape from the earth, then the spaceman got the misson to find it. I use comics style in the post to discribe this story.

We use three type of spicy to divided the district.
